Swift Mastery Guide - Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 10, 2025

Privacy Summary: Swift Mastery Guide is a privacy-first educational app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All features work entirely on your device.

Information We Don't Collect

Swift Mastery Guide does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Personal identification information
  • Camera images or video recordings
  • Audio recordings from microphone
  • Motion sensor data
  • Bluetooth device information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers

Device Permissions and Usage

Camera Access

  • Purpose: Real-time image classification demonstrations using Core ML
  • Data Processing: Images are processed entirely on your device
  • Storage: No images are saved or stored
  • Transmission: No camera data leaves your device

Microphone Access

  • Purpose: Speech-to-text demonstrations using on-device Whisper models
  • Data Processing: Audio is processed entirely on your device
  • Storage: No audio is recorded or stored
  • Transmission: No audio data leaves your device

Bluetooth Access

  • Purpose: Educational demonstration of Bluetooth device scanning
  • Data Processing: Device information displayed locally only
  • Storage: No Bluetooth data is permanently stored
  • Transmission: No Bluetooth information leaves your device

Motion Sensors

  • Purpose: Interactive tilt-based game demonstrations
  • Data Processing: Motion data used for game controls only
  • Storage: No motion data is stored
  • Transmission: No motion data leaves your device

Photo Library Access

  • Purpose: Save processed images from Core Image demonstrations
  • Data Processing: Only when user explicitly chooses to save
  • Storage: Images saved to user's photo library only
  • Transmission: No photo data transmitted

Notifications

  • Purpose: Educational demonstration of push notification features
  • Data Processing: Local notifications only
  • Storage: No notification data stored
  • Transmission: Demo notifications are local to your device

On-Device Processing

All app functionality operates entirely on your device:

  • Core ML models run locally without internet connection
  • Image processing happens in real-time on your device
  • Speech recognition uses on-device Whisper models
  • Text analysis processes content locally

Third-Party Services

Swift Mastery Guide does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your personal information. All processing happens securely on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks.

Children's Privacy

Swift Mastery Guide is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any personal information from children or adults.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in app updates with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
